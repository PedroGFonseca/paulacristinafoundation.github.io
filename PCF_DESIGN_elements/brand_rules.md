# Paula Cristina Foundation - Brand Rules

## Brand Colors

| Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|------------|----------|-------|
| Dark Blue | `#1E5E8D` | Primary - headings, buttons, backgrounds |
| Middle Blue | `#62C1DA` | Accent - links, highlights |
| Light Blue | `#D1EEFC` | Backgrounds, sections |
| Pastel Pink | `#DE9AB6` | Highlight - use sparingly |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Primary background, text on dark |

---

## Acceptable Color Combinations

### Light Blue Background
Text colors:
- Dark Blue `#1E5E8D`
- Middle Blue `#62C1DA`

### Dark Blue Background
Text colors:
- Light Blue `#D1EEFC`
- Middle Blue `#62C1DA`

### Pastel Pink Background
Text colors:
- White `#FFFFFF`

### White Background
Text colors:
- Dark Blue `#1E5E8D`
- Middle Blue `#62C1DA`
- Pastel Pink `#DE9AB6`

---

## Typography

**Font:** Montserrat (Google Fonts)
- **ExtraBold (800):** Headings (H1, H2)
- **Medium (500):** Body text, subheadings (H3, H4)

**Google Fonts CDN:**
```html
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Montserrat:wght@500;800&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
```
